Syncuity Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Syncuity uses data obtained through QuickBooks Online authorization.

1. What We Collect

We collect financial and business data from your QuickBooks Online account strictly as authorized by you. We do not collect personal user credentials or store sensitive data unnecessarily.

2. How We Use Data

Data is transferred to SQL-based reporting systems or data lakes for the purpose of reporting, analytics, and audit. We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Retention

No persistent storage of customer financial data is maintained by Syncuity unless explicitly configured by the client.

4. Third Parties

We do not share your data with third-party vendors except where required by your ETL configuration or cloud provider (e.g., Azure).

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at privacy@syncuity.com
